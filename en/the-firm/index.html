01 The story

Two cousins, one obsession.

ASTRION was born in Córdoba, from two cousins who grew up taking devices apart and asking questions: Luis Martínez and José Martínez. One fell in love with energy; the other, with software. Both, with science and artificial intelligence.

What started as family projects became an engineering firm with one simple principle: a company's energy and technology should work as a single system, with a single point of accountability.

02 Why it exists

The gap we came to close.

Most companies hire one contractor to install energy systems and another to build out technology. The two projects move forward without talking to each other, and problems show up right in the middle: in the coordination nobody owned.

ASTRION exists to close that gap. We bring solar energy, software, and artificial intelligence together under a single engineering standard and a single point of accountability. And we carry that same standard into the day-to-day: ongoing technology support so your operation never stops.
